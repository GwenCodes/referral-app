# Create a High-Converting SaaS Landing Page

Design a modern SaaS landing page for a software platform called **ReferralFlow**.

## Product Description

ReferralFlow helps contractors automatically turn completed jobs into:

* Customer referrals
* Online reviews
* Social media content
* Google Business Profile posts

Instead of hoping customers recommend them, contractors can systematically generate more word-of-mouth business from every completed project.

## Target Audience

* Remodelers
* Roofers
* Painters
* HVAC contractors
* Landscapers
* Electricians
* Plumbers
* Small construction companies

Most of these businesses currently rely on referrals and word-of-mouth for growth but lack a system to generate referrals consistently.
